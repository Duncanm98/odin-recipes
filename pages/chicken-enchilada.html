Easy Made Chicken Enchiladas

Home

Deliciously easily to make enchiladas.

This enchilada recipe makes the best, easy to make, dinner for nights when you don't want something complicated (which is every night, lets be honest). Stuffed with shredded chicken, cheese, and then topped with sauce and even more cheese - this homemade classic is an all-time favorite for a filling, easy meal.

Ingredientes

  • 2 tablespoons avocado oil or olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken (a rotisserie chicken works well for this)
  • 1 (4-ounce) can diced green chiles
  • sea salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 15 oz red enchilada sauce
  • 8-10 flour tortillas
  • 1 1/2 cups cheddar shreds
  • optional toppings: chopped fresh cilantro, chopped red onions, sliced avocado, sour cream

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven: 350°
  2. Cook Ingredients: Heat a skillet over medium heat and add oil. Sauté onion until translucent, then add the garlic and peppers and cook for about 2-3 minutes, until tender. Stir in the shredded chicken and diced green chilis, sprinkle with sea salt and pepper, and cook just long enough to heat through, then remove from heat.
  3. Grease Baking Dish: Lightly spray your baking dish with cooking spray or run butter across the dish.
  4. Assemble the Enchiladas: Gently heat the tortillas to make them easier to roll - about 10-15 seconds per side for until soft and pliable. Spread a spoonful of enchilada sauce onto the tortilla and then put a spoonful of the chicken mixture down the center, topping it with a sprinle of cheese.
  5. Roll The Tortilla: Fold over the ends where the center line of ingredients divide the tortilla and then roll it up.
  6. Repeat The Process: Go through the abovce steps for the rest of the tortilla to fill up your baking dish.
  7. Top 'em Off: Once the baking dish is full, spoon remaining enchilada sauce over each tortilla and then top it off with shredded cheese.
  8. Bake: Bake the enchiladas, uncovered, for 25 minutes. Top with any desired toppings and serve hot.

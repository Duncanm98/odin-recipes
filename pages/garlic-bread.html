Delicious 15-Minute Vegan Garlic Bread

Home

Tired of sad disappointing pre-made garlic bread?

This is the best homemade garlic bread recipes that is a guarantee to satisfy the whole family. Combining the fresh garlic with the earthiness of the ground spices & dried herbs in the olive oil and plant butter spread creates a perfect mixture soaks easily into the bread. Not every recipe has to be healthy, but they all have to be delicious, and this recipe is unhealthily delicious. All it takes is 15 minutes and you too will be able to have a wonderful pasta side dish or just an amazing snack to munch on.

Ingredientes

  • 1 baguette
  • 4 large cloves of crushed garlic
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 5 tbsp (1/3 cup) plant-based butter (margarine)
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup vegan parmesan or mozzarella (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven: 350Â°
  2. Cut Bread: Slice the baguette into 1-inch intervals
  3. Combine Ingredients:Mix together all of the ingredients, including the vegan parmesan (if using). If using mozzarella, slice thinly and add inbetween the bread slices after applying the garlic mixture.
  4. Apply Mixture: Between each slice made, drop a spoonful of the mixture in and rub it in with the back of the spoon.
  5. Use Leftover Spread: Spread the leftover butter mixture on the top of the baguette.
  6. Aluminum Foil Tent: Wrap the garlic bread loaf in aluminum foil, creating a tent by folding the foil at the top of the garlic bread - making sure that the foil is touching everywhere but the top of the loaf.
  7. Baking Time: 10 minutes
  8. Rest Time: Remove the loaf and allow it too cool down enough to handle.

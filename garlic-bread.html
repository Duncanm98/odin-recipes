Delicious 15-Minute Vegan Garlic Bread

Home

Tired of sad disappointing pre-made garlic bread?

This is the best homemade garlic bread recipes that is a guarantee to satisfy the whole family. Combining the fresh garlic with the earthiness of the ground spices & dried herbs in the olive oil and plant butter spread creates a perfect mixture soaks easily into the bread. Not every recipe has to be healthy, but they all have to be delicious, and this recipe is unhealthily delicious. All it takes is 15 minutes and you too will be able to have a wonderful pasta side dish or just an amazing snack to munch on.

Ingredientes

  • 1 baguette
  • 4 large cloves of crushed garlic
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 5 tbsp (1/3 cup) plant-based butter (margarine)
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup vegan parmesan or mozzarella (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat over to 350Â°
  2. Slice the baguette into 1-inch intervals
  3. Mix together all of the ingredients include in the vegan parmesan (if using). If using mozzarella, slince thinly and add in between the bread slices after applying the garlic mixture.
  4. Using a teaspoon, slight spread two slices and drop a spponful of the mixture in, rubbing it around with the back of the spoon.
  5. Spoon leftover butter garlic mix on top of the baguette.
  6. Wrap the loaf in aluminum foil, tenting and folding the foil at the top of the garlic bread so that it's touching the bottom and sides of the baguette but not the top.
  7. Bake for 10 minutes.
  8. Remove and allow to cool enough to handle and serve!
